Privacy Policy

Puppy Prep ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application that helps dog breeders and owners manage pregnancy, whelping, and neonatal care for their dogs. This policy explains what information the app collects, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What we collect

Stored only on your device (never sent to us)

  • The breed, status, mating/birth dates, dam weight and age band, puppy count, and nursing status you enter during onboarding.
  • Your generated care calendar — events, completion marks, snooze states.
  • Puppy profiles: nickname, ID colour, birth weight, sex (optional).
  • Weight logs for each puppy.

This data is stored locally on your device using encrypted on-device storage (MMKV). It does not leave your device unless you purchase Puppy Prep Pro or sign in, both optional.

Collected only if you sign up or pay

  • Email address — account recovery and receipt delivery.
  • Purchase receipt — managed by Apple / Google; we never see your payment card.
  • Anonymised crash reports and basic usage analytics (opt out in Settings).

We do NOT collect

  • Your location.
  • Your contacts.
  • Photos or camera content.
  • Microphone audio.
  • Advertising identifiers for tracking.
  • Health data about humans.

2. How we use the information

  • Local data is used solely to generate your personalised care calendar and weight-tracking charts on your device.
  • Email (if provided) is used only for account recovery and receipts. No marketing unless you opt in.
  • Crash / analytics data is anonymised and used only to fix bugs.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers.

4. Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Export your data — Settings → Privacy → Export my data.
  • Delete everything — Settings → Privacy → Delete my account (cancels active subscription).
  • Opt out of analytics — Settings → Privacy → Disable analytics.

Under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and PDPA (Singapore), you may also request corrections or a copy of any personal data we hold. Email privacy@puppyprep.app — we respond within 30 days.

5. Children

Puppy Prep is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Security

Data on your device is encrypted using iOS Keychain / Android Keystore where supported. Data in transit uses TLS 1.3.

7. Changes to this policy

We will post any changes here and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes are communicated via an in-app notice before they take effect.
