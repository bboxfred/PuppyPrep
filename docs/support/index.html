Support

Puppy Prep is built by a solo developer in Singapore. Every support email is read personally, usually within 24 hours.

Email: support@puppyprep.app

Common questions

The app says my breed isn't supported.

Choose "Mixed Breed / Not Sure" during onboarding — we'll use size-based generic guidelines. Then email us the breed name and we'll add it in the next update.

I want to reset everything and start fresh.

Uninstall and reinstall. All local data is cleared. Your purchase will restore via "Settings → Restore Purchases".

How do I cancel / refund Puppy Prep Pro?

Pro is a one-time purchase, not a subscription — there's nothing to cancel. Refund requests go through Apple Report a Problem (iOS) or Google Play Order History (Android).

I found a bug / my schedule doesn't match my vet's advice.

Please email us with the breed, your dog's status, and what you expected to see vs what you got. Screenshots help. We take accuracy very seriously.

Is this a subscription?

No. Puppy Prep Pro is a one-time purchase. You own it forever. Library stays free whether you pay or not.
