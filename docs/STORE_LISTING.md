## ✉️ RESPONSES TO REVIEWER QUESTIONS

Apple App Review sometimes asks questions during review. Pre-written answers:

### "Why do you need notifications?"
> Puppy Prep generates a personalised care calendar for dog breeders. Users set their preferred notification time during onboarding. Notifications remind them of scheduled care tasks like deworming doses, vaccinations, feeding windows, and vet visits. Missing one of these can harm or kill a puppy (e.g., hypoglycaemia in toy breeds can cause death within hours of a missed feeding). Notifications are the core reason users install the app — they're not marketing or promotional, they're medical reminders.

### "What does your app do?"
> Puppy Prep is an educational and organisational tool for dog breeders and owners. It asks 12 questions about their dog and litter, then generates a personalised day-by-day care calendar drawing on veterinary-reviewed sources. The app includes a free Birth Guide and Emergency Guide, and an optional one-time in-app purchase unlocks the complete calendar plus weight tracking for individual puppies.

### "Is this medical advice?"
> No. Puppy Prep is an educational tool. Every health event includes the guidance "always consult your vet for medical decisions." The Emergency Guide is a first-aid reference for the minutes before a user reaches professional veterinary care — not a replacement. The Terms of Service make this explicit.
