# Privacy Policy — Puppy Prep

**Last updated: [YYYY-MM-DD]**
**Effective date: [YYYY-MM-DD]**

Puppy Prep ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile application that helps dog breeders and owners manage pregnancy, whelping, and neonatal care for their dogs. This policy explains what information the app collects, how we use it, and your rights.

## 1. What we collect

### Stored only on your device (never sent to us)
- The breed, status, mating/birth dates, dam weight and age band, puppy count, and nursing status that you enter during onboarding.
- Your generated care calendar — all events, completion marks, and snooze states.
- Puppy profiles you create: nickname, ID colour, birth weight, sex (optional).
- Individual puppy weight logs.

This data is stored locally on your device using encrypted on-device storage (MMKV). It **does not leave your device** unless you purchase Puppy Prep Pro or sign in, which are both optional.

### Collected only if you sign up or pay
- Email address (for account recovery and receipt delivery).
- Purchase receipt (managed by Apple App Store / Google Play — we do not see your payment card details).
- Anonymised crash reports and basic usage analytics so we can fix bugs (opt-out in Settings).

### We do NOT collect
- Your location.
- Your contacts.
- Photos or camera content (we do not request access).
- Microphone audio.
- Advertising identifiers for tracking.
- Health data about humans.

## 2. How we use the information

- **Local data** is used solely to generate your personalised care calendar and weight-tracking charts on your device.
- **Email** (if provided) is used only for account recovery and to send purchase receipts. We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in.
- **Crash / analytics data** is used to identify and fix bugs. It is anonymised — we cannot trace it back to individual users.

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** share your data with advertisers.

## 3. Third-party services we use

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| Apple App Store / Google Play | Payment processing, subscription management | Purchase receipt only — payment details never pass through us |
| RevenueCat (optional) | Subscription entitlement tracking | Anonymous device ID + entitlement status |
| PostHog (optional, self-hosted) | Anonymous crash + usage analytics | Anonymous device ID + crash stack traces |

## 4. Your rights

You can, at any time:
- **See everything the app has about you**: Settings → Privacy → Export my data.
- **Delete all data**: Settings → Privacy → Delete my account (this also cancels any active subscription).
- **Opt out of analytics**: Settings → Privacy → Disable analytics.

Under GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and PDPA (Singapore), you also have the right to request a copy of any personal data we hold, request corrections, and request deletion. Email us at **privacy@puppyprep.app** with any request and we will respond within 30 days.

## 5. Children

Puppy Prep is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 6. Security

Data on your device is encrypted using iOS Keychain / Android Keystore where supported. Data in transit (when you sign in or pay) uses TLS 1.3.

## 7. Changes to this policy

We will post any changes to this page and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice before they take effect.
